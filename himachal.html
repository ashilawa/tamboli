Himachal Tour

TOUR NAMEDURATION
Himachal Tour6 Nights/ 7 Days

-- HIMACHAL --



Day 01:DELHI—SHIMLA (APPROX. 308 KMS / 08 HRS)

Arrive Delhi departure for Shimla. Departure for local sightseeing in Shimla ( The Mall, Lakkar Bazaar and Scandal Point). Dinner at resort. Overnight at resort.

Day 02:SHIMLA

After breakfast, departure for full day sightseeing to Chail ( Chail Palace, World’s highest Cricket ground, Sidh Temple and Kali Ka Tibba) and Kufri ( Deshu Peak and Meshu Peak). Dinner at resort in Shimla. Overnight at resort in Shimla.

Day 03:SHIMLA-MANALI (APPROX. 267 KMS / 06 HRS)

After breakfast, check out from resort and departure for Manali. Check in at hotel. Evening free for rest or personal activities. Dinner at hotel. Overnight at hotel.

Day 04:MANALI – ROHTANG PASS – MANALI (AT YOUR OWN COST)

After breakfast, departure for full day sightseeing tour to Rohtang Pass / Snow Point (Enroute sightseeing to Nehru Kund, Kothi, Rahall a Falls and Marhi). Dinner at hotel in Manali. Overnight at hotel in Manali. @Rohtang Pass is closed on Tuesday.

Day 05:MANALI

After breakfast, Departure for half day local sightseeing in Manali ( Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Buddhist Monastery, Club House and Vashisht Bath).. Dinner at hotel in Manali. Overnight at hotel in Manali.

Day 06:MANALI-CHANDIGARH (APPROX. 270 KMS / 09 HRS)

After breakfast drive for Chandigarh. Check in at hotel / resort. Departure for sightseeing tour to Sukhna Lake ( boating at your own cost) and Rock Garden. Enjoy shopping at Sector 17 market (optional). Overnight at hotel.

Day 07:CHANDIGARH—DELHI (APPROX. 249 KMS / 05 HRS)

After breakfast, check out from hotel & proceed to go back home with sweet Memories of the trip!!!!!!!!!!!

PACKAGE INCLUDES :

- Two night stay at Shimla with two breakfast and two dinner.
- Three night stay at Manali with three breakfast and three dinner.
- One night stay at Chandigarh with one breakfast and one dinner.
- Vehicle from Delhi to Delhi on disposal.
- Sightseeing as mentioned above.
- Transportation cost includes vehicle charges, driver allowances, fuel, state tax, toll tax and parking.
- All Applicable Hotel Taxes.

PACKAGE COST DOESNT INCLUDE:-

- Expenses of personal nature such as drinks, telephone, and laundry bills etc.
- Tips and porter charges.
- Camera fees.
Any additional expenses incurred due to any flight delay or cancellation, weather conditions, political closures, technical faults etc. Any other services/activities not specified above.
