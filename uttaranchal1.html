-- UTTARANCHAL --



Day 01: Delhi – Nainital (310kms/7-8Hrs)

Arrive Delhi, proceed to Nainital – the beautiful lake district of Kumaon. Check-in to your hotel and freshen up. The Kumaon hills are known for their idyllic beauty – mountains silhouetted against the sky, streams rippling through the silence of forests, and placid lakes reflecting the changing panorama of nature. In the evening, enjoy a boat ride on the famous lake and visit the local market for some shopping. Overnight stay at Nainital.

Day 02: Nainital

After breakfast the day will be spent doing local sightseeing in and around Naintal. There are many places of interest like the Naina Devi Temple, Lands’ End, Tiffin Top and the Zoo. One can also take the ropeway (Directly payable by the customer) to the snow view point. You can also do boating on the Naini Lake. Spend the evening at leisure and stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 03: Nainital – Kausani (120kms/4hr)

Drive to Kausani, at the height of 6250 ft. / 1890 meters above sea level, it offers a spectacular 300 kms wide view of the Himalayas & walks in the thickly wooded area. Check in Hotel. Later at evening, enjoy the serene beauty of this beautiful hilly resort visiting tea gardens and grassy meadows of Kausani. Overnight stay at Kausani.

Day 04: Kausani

Early morning you can see the enchanting sunrise over the Himalayas -a rich experience, which will remain alive in your memories for lifetime. After Breakfast, excursions to the 12th Century Baijnath Temple by the Gomti River & also visit Bageshwar Temple. Return back to Kausani. Overnight stay.

Day 05: Kausani – Ranikhet - Corbett

Morning proceed to reach Ranikhet. Drive through the spectacularly changing vistas, climbing up close to the snow capped peaks. Ranikhet :6000 ft. / 1820 meters above sea level it a serene and peaceful hill station foreshadowed by conifer forests and surrounded by snow clad Himalayan ranges from the Bagirathi peaks in the west to Nanda Kot in the East. Ranikhet also offers golfing at the 9 hole army course beside being known for the orchards of Chaubatia and Jhoola Devi Temple. After Ranikhet sight-seeing proceed to Corbett National Park. The park was named after Jim Corbett, the legendary hunter-naturalist turned author and photographer who spent most years of his life in this area and helped set up this park. Check into Resort, enjoy recreational facilities at Resort. Overnight stay at Corbett.

Day 06: Corbett

Early morning you can enjoy safari (At additional cost ) inside the Wildlife Sanctuary for your tryst with the big cats. Elephants are also available for dense jungle safaris on a first come first serve basis. Those interested in angling can try hooking the mahseer fish for sport. Birding treks and horse safaris are also exciting options for the enthusiasts. Back to Resort, Overnight stay at Corbett.

Day 07: Corbett –Delhi (260kms/6-7hrs)

Breakfast at Hotel. Depart for Delhi by surface. On arrival at Delhi transfer to Airport Station.
