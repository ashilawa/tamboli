TOUR NAME DURATION
Golden Triangle 4 Nights/ 5 Days

-- GOLDEN TRIANGLE --



[ Delhi, Agra, Jaipur ]



Day 1: Delhi (Capital of India).

Arrival at Delhi airport/ railway station. Then transfer to hotel after fresh n up enjoy the sightseeing of Delhi. Evening back to hotel. Overnight at Delhi.

Day 2 : Agra :

Drive to Agra, On arrival check into hotel. Later enjoy the sightseeing of Agra. Overnight at Agra.

Day 3 : Jaipur :

Drive to Jaipur. Enroute visit Fathepur Sikri. On arrival check into hotel at jaipur. Overnight at Jaipur .

Day 4 : Jaipur :

In morning enjoy the sightseeing of Pink City Jaipur. Do shopping in local market, Overnight at Hotel .

Day 5 : Delhi :

Drive to Delhi. On arrival drop at Airport or Railway Station.

-- RAJASTHAN --



[ Mount Abu, Udaipur, Ajmer, Pushkar, Jaipur ]



Day 1: Mont abu :

Arrive Abu road railway station , pick up and transfer to Mount Abu Check into the hotel on arrival. Rest of the day at leisure.

Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2 : Mount Abu :

Take a full day sightseeing tour and visit Dilwara Jain Temples, Prajapita Brahmakumari Ashram and University, Gurusikhar and Nakki Lake. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: Udaipur (Lake City) :

From Abu transfer to Udaipur, On arrival check into the hotel. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4 : Udaipur :

Take a full day sightseeing tour and visit the City Palace, Saheliyon ki Badi, Maharana Pratap Samarak and Pear Hill. Overnight stay at the hotel .

Day 5: Ajmer/ Pushkar :

After breakfast proceed to Pushkar via Ajmer. Enjoy Sight seeing of Ajmer and pushkar visit Khwaja shah Dargah, Brahma temple and Puskar Lake. Overnight stay at the hotel .

Day 6: Jaipur : (Pink City)

Drive to Jaipur , On arrival check into hotel to. In evening go for Chokhi dhani visit (Optional). Rest day at leisure. Overnight at Jaipur.

Day 7 : Jaipur :

Take a full day sightseeing tour of Pink City and visit Amber Fort, Birla Manidr, Hawa Mahal, City Palace and Jantar Mantar, observatory. Overnight at Hotel .

Day 8 : Jaipur:

Drop at Railway station or Airport.
